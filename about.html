About TechNova Solutions

Building reliable, scalable software for businesses that refuse to settle for "good enough."

Our Story

From a two-person team to a growing technology partner

TechNova Solutions was founded in 2018 in a small co-working space in Bengaluru by two engineers who were frustrated with how slowly traditional IT vendors moved. They believed software companies could be both technically excellent and genuinely easy to work with.

Today, TechNova designs and ships web platforms, mobile apps, cloud infrastructure, and AI-powered features for startups and established enterprises alike — without losing the responsiveness of the small team we started as.

🎯

Our Mission

To help ambitious businesses turn their ideas into dependable, well-engineered software — delivered with transparency, speed, and genuine technical craftsmanship.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted technology partner for growing companies, known as much for our integrity as for the quality of what we build.

Our Journey

Cloud & DevOps practice formed

2024

AI & Data Analytics division
