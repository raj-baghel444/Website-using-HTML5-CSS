About TechNova Solutions

Building reliable, simple software for businesses that need it to just work.

Our Story

From a small team to a trusted technology partner

TechNova Solutions was founded by a small team of engineers who wanted to build software companies could actually rely on. We started by building websites for local businesses, and have grown into a team that builds websites, apps, and software for clients across India.

We've kept the same principle since day one: build things that work, communicate honestly, and treat every client's project like our own.
